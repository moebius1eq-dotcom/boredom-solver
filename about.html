About BoredomSolver

Our Mission

Welcome to BoredomSolver, the only website that's completely honest about the fact that sometimes you just don't want to do anything productive. And you know what? That's perfectly fine.

We created BoredomSolver because we're tired of websites that immediately respond to boredom with "learn a new skill!" or "call your grandmother!" or "do 50 jumping jacks!" Sure, those things are great. But sometimes you just want to stare at something weird, scroll through nonsensical content, and embrace the void.

Who We Are

BoredomSolver was built by people who understand the art of doing absolutely nothing. We're not anti-productivity ‚Äì we're pro-choice. Sometimes the most productive thing you can do is absolutely nothing at all.

Our team consists of professional procrastinators, digital philosophers, and experts in the field of wasting time meaningfully. We've all been there: staring at the ceiling, wondering why we're like this, and wishing someone would just give us something weird to do without judgment.

  • No "call a friend" suggestions ‚Äì Sometimes you don't want to talk to anyone. That's valid.

Why Boredom Matters

Boredom is actually a useful psychological state. Research shows that boredom can boost creativity, enhance problem-solving, and give your brain necessary rest. Rather than fighting boredom, we embrace it.

So the next time someone gives you grief for "doing nothing," tell them you're practicing mindful boredom. You're welcome.
