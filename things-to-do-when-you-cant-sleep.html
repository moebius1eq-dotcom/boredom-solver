Things to Do When You Can't Sleep

Staring at the ceiling at 2 AM? Can't sleep but don't want to just lie there? We've all been there. Here are things to do when you can't sleep that won't leave you more tired tomorrow.

üò¥ Why Can't You Sleep?

Whether it's stress, anxiety, too much screen time, or just a racing mind - insomnia hits everyone. Instead of tossing and turning, try these activities.

üéÆ Things to Do When You Can't Sleep

Play Idle Games

Low-effort games that play themselves are perfect when you're too tired to think but too awake to sleep. Perfect for those middle-of-the-night boredom moments.

Listen to Podcasts or Audiobooks

Put on something boring (yes, really!) or a familiar comfort show. The distraction helps quiet your mind while being relaxing.

Read Something Boring

That textbook you've been putting off? Now's perfect. Dry technical manuals work wonders for making you sleepy.

Try Guided Meditation

Evening meditation and breathing exercises can help calm your mind. Try sleep meditations or simply focus on your breathing.

Plan Tomorrow

Make a mental list of what you need to do tomorrow. Getting it out of your head and onto "paper" can help stop the racing thoughts.

üßò Sleep-Friendly Activities

  • Progressive muscle relaxation
  • Deep breathing exercises (4-7-8 technique)
  • Listening to ambient sounds or rain sounds
  • Gentle stretching in bed
  • Journaling your thoughts

‚ö†Ô∏è What NOT to Do When You Can't Sleep

  • Don't check your phone - the blue light makes it worse
  • Don't watch exciting content - save the thrillers for daytime
  • Don't stress about not sleeping - it makes it harder
  • Don't drink caffeine after 2 PM

Remember: if you can't sleep, getting up and doing something quiet is better than lying there stressing. These activities can help tire your brain without keeping you awake.
